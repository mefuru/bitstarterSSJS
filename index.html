My name is Mehul Mandania, I'm based in London and I've got a keen interest in health and fitness. I'm fascinated by technology and I have been taking Stanford's Startup Engineering MOOC on Coursera for the last couple of months on a part time basis. The final project involves creating a Bitcoin selfstarter to:

  1. Exhibit our newly found web development skills
  2. Validate a business idea
  3. Have fun!

Well, you've arrived at my Bitcoin selfstarter!

Whilst going through the INCREDIBLE material for the course, I asked myself "Wouldn't it be great if we could use the technology used to create MOOCs to transform the health and fitness industry?"

MOOCs give great educators an opportunity to share their gift of teaching to a huge number of students. Online Fitness gives great coaches and trainers the same opportunity.

Our aim is to allow anyone with an internet connection to "Take fitness courses from the world's best trainers, for free". We want to give users access to a large choice of great training courses, which can be taken in a community environment.

About

How does it work?

  1. Find a course that meets your fitness goals
  2. Sign-up for free
  3. Follow the instructions
  4. Make friends, discuss the course and organise meetups on our forums
  5. Become fitter!

Who's it for?

We strive to create courses that will match whatever your fitness need. Our initial courses will allow you to:

  1. Decrease bodyfat
  2. Build muscle mass
  3. Improve cardio-vascular fitness
  4. Increase flexibility/Yoga
  5. General well being
  6. Connect with like minded people

What will the donations be used for?

Donations will be used to pay for hosting, create original world class content and to guage interest of the public. If we do not reach our target, all donations will be returned.
